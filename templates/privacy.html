Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 June 2026

My Mom's Stories is operated by LOVEUAD LTD (Company No. 16838046), registered in England & Wales, based in Leeds, UK — the data controller for the personal data described below. Contact: privacy@mymomsstories.com.

What we collect

  • Parent account: email address, hashed password (never stored in plain text), country, chosen heritage language.
  • Child account: a nickname you choose (we ask you not to use a real name), a username and hashed password you set, country, language.
  • Activity records: which words/lines/customs/stories a child attempted, the text transcript of what they said, and a score. We do not store voice recordings. Speech is converted to text in the browser (or sent to Google's Gemini API for scoring) and only the resulting text and score are saved.
  • Usage minutes: how many minutes a child account is active each month, to track the 15-hour included usage and any overage.
  • Billing: handled entirely by Stripe — we never see or store card details. We keep a record of subscription status and payment events (amount, date, success/failure) for accounting.
  • Site analytics: Microsoft Clarity, on parent-facing and public pages only. It is deliberately excluded from any page a child is using.

Why we process this data

To provide the service you've signed up for (contract), to charge the subscription you've agreed to (contract), and for the legitimate interest of understanding how the product is used so we can improve it (analytics, on parent-facing pages only).

Children's data

Child accounts are created and controlled by a parent — children do not sign up themselves. We ask that real names not be used as the child's nickname. Activity transcripts and scores exist to show parents their child's progress; they are visible only to that child's parent and to LOVEUAD LTD for support purposes.

Where your data is processed

Reading attempts are scored using Google's Gemini API, which may process data outside the UK/EEA under Google's standard contractual clauses. Payment is processed by Stripe. Hosting is via our cloud infrastructure provider.

How long we keep it

For as long as the account is active. If you delete your account, your data is permanently removed — see below.

Your rights

  • Access: everything a child has done is already visible on the parent dashboard's activity log.
  • Erasure: use "Clear data" on any child's card to wipe just that child's activity history, or "Delete account" on your dashboard to permanently remove your account and every child account under it. This cannot be undone.
  • Rectification: you can change a child's login username and password from the parent dashboard at any time.
  • Portability / a copy of your data: email privacy@mymomsstories.com and we'll send what we hold on your account.
  • Objection / complaint: contact us first; you can also complain to the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ico.org.uk).

Cookies

We use a session cookie to keep you logged in (essential, no consent needed) and Microsoft Clarity for analytics on non-child pages. No advertising cookies are used.

Contact

LOVEUAD LTD, Leeds, United Kingdom. privacy@mymomsstories.com
